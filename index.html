About Our Institute

A Sanctuary of Sacred Knowledge

TWAREEQUL IHSAAN FOUNDATION (TIF) is a dedicated center for Qur'anic and Islamic education serving the Muslim community of northern Malawi. We blend authentic traditional teaching with a caring, modern environment.

From beginner Qira'ah (recitation) to the complete memorization of the Holy Qur'an, our students are guided by qualified scholars in an atmosphere of discipline, tarbiyah (character building) and brotherhood. We welcome boys, girls, men and women, every soul seeking the light of revelation.

Our Mission

To spread authentic Islamic knowledge and produce God-conscious learners of strong character.

Our Vision

A generation rooted in the Qur'an & Sunnah, contributing positively to society.
